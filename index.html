✦ Features

Everything your campus needs

From event discovery to club management — designed for students, clubs, and admins alike.

🔍
Smart Event Discovery
Browse, search, and filter events by category. Find exactly what interests you — Technical, Cultural, Sports, Hackathons, and more.
📬
Email Notifications
Get confirmation emails after registering and reminders 1 day before events. Receive alerts for events matching your interest profile.
🏢
Club Dashboards
Club admins can post events, upload posters, view registrations, and send announcements — all from a dedicated dashboard.
🎓
Student Dashboard
See upcoming events, registered activities, and recommendations based on your selected interest areas.
🔐
Secure Authentication
JWT-based auth with bcrypt password encryption. Separate login flows for students, club admins, and super admins.
📊
Analytics Panel
Super admins get a full overview — total students, clubs, events, most active clubs, and popular event categories.

✦ How It Works

From signup to event — in seconds

Getting started is simple. No confusion, no friction.

02

Explore Clubs & Events

Browse all active clubs on campus, see upcoming events, and get recommendations tailored to you.

03

Register & Get Notified

Register for events with one click. Receive a confirmation email instantly and a reminder 24 hours before.

04

Attend events, build your campus network, collect certificates, and never miss an opportunity again.

✦ User Roles

Built for everyone on campus

Three distinct roles, each with a dedicated dashboard and full set of tools.

🎓
Student
  • Sign up & select interest domains
  • Explore clubs and browse events
  • Register with one click
  • Get email confirmations & reminders
  • Personalized recommendations
  • View event details (venue, date, time)
🏢
Club Admin
  • Create & manage club profile
  • Post new events with posters
  • Add full event details
  • View list of registered students
  • Send club announcements
  • Manage participation fees
👑
Super Admin
  • Approve club registrations
  • Manage all users & accounts
  • Monitor and moderate events
  • Remove inappropriate content
  • Platform-wide analytics
  • Full control over the platform
