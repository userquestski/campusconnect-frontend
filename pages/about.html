✦ Our Story

Built for campus life,
by students who get it.

CampusConnect was born from a simple frustration — great campus events going unnoticed, brilliant clubs struggling to reach students. We built the solution.

✦ Our Mission

One platform. Every opportunity.

We believe no student should miss an event that could change their career, spark a passion, or build a lifelong friendship — just because they didn't know about it.

CampusConnect brings together event discovery, club management, and smart notifications so every student is always in the loop — automatically.

✦ What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🌐
Inclusivity First
Every student deserves access to campus life — regardless of department, year, or background. We build for everyone.
⚡
Zero Friction
From discovering an event to registering for it — one click. We remove every unnecessary step from the student experience.
🔒
Privacy & Security
Student data is never sold or shared. JWT auth, bcrypt passwords, and strict role-based access protect every account.
📣
Empowering Clubs
Clubs are the heartbeat of campus. We give every club admin the tools to run professional events without technical overhead.
🎯
Smart Discovery
Interest-based recommendations mean students see events relevant to them — not just a firehose of every campus activity.
🚀
Open to All
CampusConnect is designed to be deployed by any college. Open, accessible, and built to scale across entire universities.
